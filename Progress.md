# Fetures

## Lexical Analyser

**The Lexical Analizer supports Tokens bellow.**

---

- **Delimiters:**

  - `(`
  - `)`
  - `{`
  - `}`
  - `,`
  - `;`
  - `.`

---

- **Arithmetic Operators:**

  - `+`
  - `-`
  - `*`
  - `/`

---

- **Locgial Operators:**

  - `!`
  - `!=`
  - `=`
  - `==`
  - `<`
  - `<=`
  - `>`
  - `>=`

---

- **Literals:**

  - `String`

---

- **Ignores:**

  - `//`
  - ` `
  - `\t`
  - `\r`
  - `\n`

---

- **If token does not match any of above, then that recognized as Invalid Token.**
